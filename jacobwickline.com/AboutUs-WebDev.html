About Us

Come and relax in the mountains of southern West Virginia. Experience the atmosphere of small town Appalachian hospitality, and take time to reflect on the beauty of God's earth

The View B&B is the newly remodeled home of Elizabeth Muldoon and her family. It is located on seven acres of land at the edge of small town Athens, WV, only minutes from Pipestem State Park, Winterplace ski resort and Concord University. Residing on the ridge provides a peaceful view of the valley and Athens Lake from the back patio and deck. Our home graciously accommodates persons with disabilities. Three rooms are available with a variety of amenities including wireless internet access at affordable prices. Children of all ages are welcome
